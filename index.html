OVERVIEW

India is the fastest-growing country for tech spending in Asia Pacific, increasing by 8 per cent in 2019 and by 7 per cent in 2020, Forrester. The report says that India’s hunger for software will continue in 2019 and 2020 as the digital transformation of government and private-sector business processes remains atop India’s agenda. Spending on tech consulting and outsourcing services will bolster IT expenditure growth in the country, increasing by 11 per cent in 2019 and 16 per cent in 2020.

In line with this vision, The World CIO 200 Summit 2023 will bring together the digital leaders who have gone the extra mile in living up to the expectations of their organization and thereby the nation.
